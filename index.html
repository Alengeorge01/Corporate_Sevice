“Best Combination : SMART & SKILLED”

In this competitive world, when everyone is aiming to be the best, it requires a lot of courage and patience to nurture the creative side of oneself. Creativity is not something which can be learnt in a crash course or by sitting in a classroom, It needs to be developed by understanding yourself better each day. One can be smart in everything, but not necessarily skilled in everything. One of the best combinations which is rarely found is to be smart and skilled for the required task. This reminds me of the Lord Jesus Christ who was not only smart but was skilled as well, how can I say that? Let’s read Mark 6:2, it says: (When the Sabbath came, he began to teach in the synagogue, and many who heard him were amazed. "Where did this man get these things?" they asked. "What's this wisdom that has been given him? What are these remarkable miracles he is performing?) HE first spoke WISDOM, which amazed people there. Not only was He smart, He also was skilled in what HE was called to do. No one can categorize Lord Jesus Christ as a mythology character, nor can anyone deny His existence, Why am I so sure about His reality? Because even if you refuse to believe about Him through the bible, history is a proof of it, as its divided into BC & AD all revolving around Jesus Christ. Jesus Christ was smart, HE was full of wisdom & was skilled in performing the miracles. Now many will want to know the source of His wisdom & skill, We can read it in John 14:10 where Lord Jesus Christ is having a conversation with his disciples : (Don’t you believe that I am in the Father and the Father is in me? The words I speak are not my own, but my Father who lives in me does his work through me.) HE considered His father as the true source of His wisdom & skill. We can read the same thing in the book of Colossians 1:27 where the apostle Paul says, : (For God wanted them to know that the riches and glory of Christ are for you Gentiles, too. And this is the secret: Christ lives in you. This gives you assurance of sharing his glory.) This was the secret that “Christ lives in you”, so no one can stop you as well to be smart and skilled, because now you know the true source. It is important to understand the reality with an open mind rather than to try finding the truth with a closed one.

~ Sydney Fernandes
B.E (Civil) 2005-09

~ Poorvick James
B. Tech(ECE) 2015-19

“LIFE- Win and Learn ”

There are many experiences in our lives. But which one do we generally appreciate- ‘The Win’ or ‘The Loose’? Obviously ‘The Win’. But why is the factor ‘The Loose’ not appreciated. Cause we don’t want that pain, that shame, that suffering in our lives. Some say, ‘Bro, no one should suffer that failure in their lives like how I suffered. It would be sometimes bitter for us. But what is the whole purpose of it. Nothing comes in lives for no reason and go. Every work in our lives has a purpose. That’s when I recall the concept ‘The Period of Incubation’. Every human have a learning period in their lives. Some have long learning period whereas some have less learning period; depends on perception.
Now let me tell you something that I learnt in my Soft Computing class. That subject was about Machine Learning. Technically, a machine is said to be learnt only when it undergoes several number of iteration process. Same way sometimes we need to go through new experiences in life for becoming a learnt person. This is called as Period of incubation, the place where a person starts to grow. Let me give you examples of great personalities from Bible- David, Joshua, Job, Esther and many more. Everyone had that period of Incubation. But what made them different than us is their point of view towards the problems. In life problems come and go, generally people say it’s the probability of ½ but I say the probability is 2/2 . They say we win or we lose, but I say it’s we Win and we Learn. If we win it’s good, if you didn’t then we learn from it. Always remember Victory is always Yours. Paul writes a letter to the Corinthian church, he says ‘But thanks be to God! He gives us the victory through our Lord Jesus Christ.’(1 Corinthians 15:57). Jesus clearly told ‘ it’s finished’ in John 19:30. Every battle in our lives is finished. The victory is already been declared. He had conquered Death; death in all our circumstances of life. He gave Life and declared VICTORY for all of us. So that we will no longer be a slave, but a Conqueror. We no longer are weak, but strong in Him. We are not a failure, but we are Victors because Christ is our Victor.

~Poorvick James
B. Tech(ECE) 2015-19
